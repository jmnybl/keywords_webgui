Given a space separated list of keywords (topic) this demo computes typical words occuring in the context of your keywords. You can either have one keyword group and calculate it agains a random text sample, or have two or more keyword groups and calculate agains each other. Try for example two groups: Kreikka and Venäjä, and you will get words typical in the context of Greece and words typical in the context of Russia.










Technical details

The demo is based on a linear support vector classifier using the scikit-learn python library, and the training data is pooled from the Finnish Internet Parsebank or the Suomi24 corpus. Training data consists of the sentences containing at least one of the user defined keywords, and the classifier is trained to predict which keyword group a given sentence belongs to based on the other words in the sentence. Keyword lists are then produced by picking words which give the highest positive signal for a particular group.

The software behind the demo is open-source, available on GitHub. The demo is maintained by the Turku BioNLP group.
